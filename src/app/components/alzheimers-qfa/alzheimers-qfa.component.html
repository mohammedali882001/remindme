Preparation and Awareness for Alzheimer's Disease

Early symptoms of Alzheimer's include memory loss, difficulty concentrating, and problems with routine tasks. Recognizing these signs early can lead to better management and care.

Supporting someone with Alzheimer's involves understanding their needs, being patient, and providing emotional and practical support to improve their quality of life.

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that causes brain cells to degenerate and die, leading to a continuous decline in thinking, behavioral, and social skills.
